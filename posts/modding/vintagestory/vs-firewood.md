# Vintage Story - Firewood Conversions Mods

I made a couple of mods for Vintage Story that allows you to chop quartered and and debarked logs to firewood. These are very simple json recipe mods. Each one takes 1x debarked/quartered log and provides 4x firewood.

- [Debarked Logs to Firewood](https://mods.vintagestory.at/tzdebarkedfirewood)
- [Quartered Logs to Firewood](https://mods.vintagestory.at/tzquarteredfirewood)

repo link for my vintage story mods: [https://github.com/twizzork/vintagestory-mods](https://github.com/twizzork/vintagestory-mods)
